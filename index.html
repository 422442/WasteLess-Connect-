About WasteLess Connect

Our Mission

WasteLess Connect aims to reduce food waste and hunger by connecting surplus food sources with those who need it most. We believe in the power of communities to drive sustainable change and ensure that good food doesn't go to waste.

How It Started

Founded by a group of food enthusiasts and sustainability advocates, our journey began with the realization that tons of edible food are discarded daily while many go hungry.

Through technology and community partnerships, we strive to make food sharing accessible and impactful for everyone.
